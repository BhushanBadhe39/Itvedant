My Favourite Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • spices:
    • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
    • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Mix flour,sugar and butter.
  3. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Stir in spices and baking powder.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.

Cooking Terms

Knead
Work dough by hand to develop gluten.
Whisk
Beat Ingredients quikly using a whisk.
Simmer
Cook just below boiling point.
